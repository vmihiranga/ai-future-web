Why AI is Transforming the World

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing industries by enabling machines to learn, reason, and make decisions. According to McKinsey, AI could contribute up to $15.7 trillion to the global economy by 2030. From diagnosing diseases in healthcare to optimizing supply chains in logistics, AI's applications are vast and transformative.

The current AI revolution is driven by three key factors: exponential growth in computing power, vast amounts of available data, and breakthrough algorithms like deep learning and transformers. Organizations worldwide are adopting AI to enhance productivity, reduce costs, and create new business models that were previously impossible.
